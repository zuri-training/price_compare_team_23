FAQ

Here are some of the ShopMore frequently asked questions.
Q: Is Your Website Secure?

A: Our website is secured with SSL certificate. This means that the information you input is encrypted and will not be available for third parties.

Q: Why should I buy on ShopMore?

A: Saves you the stress. By ordering online, you will get prices faster and you will be able to go through order confirmation and payment process much faster.

Q: How to contact customer service?

A: If you have questions regarding our online store (account questions, technical questions) please contact support@shopmore.com or call +234 464 8128.

Q: Do you have the product in stock?

A: All products shown on our site are available. Pending the time of your order is dependent on the products and quantities.

Q: How can I order?

A: You can order easily using our online platform. When you find a product you need, you will be linked to the site of purchase where you can order with ease.

Q: Are the brands authentic?

A: Our brands are authentic and sourced from trust-worthy sites. If you are buying from websites that you are not familiar with, research the products they offer.

Q: How do I change my personal details?

A: You can easily change all your information on your account. When you login to ShopMore, go to your account and edit. This will allow you change all your contact information.

Q: How can I save when shopping?

A: One of the benefits of shopping online is that you are able to find products at lower prices due to the economics of E-commerce. We recommend signing up to our newsletter for updates on sales and new products.

Still have questions?

If you cannot find an answer to your question in our FAQ, you can always Contact Us. We will attend to you shortly!
